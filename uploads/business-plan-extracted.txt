[P]1.  Executive Summary

[P]

[P]AMPM Group Limited is a compliance-led building services and facilities management group, headquartered in the south of England. The Group is being built to consolidate fire safety, passive fire protection, mechanical and electrical (M&amp;E) services, and facilities management into a single, tech-enabled offering for the UK mid-market.

[P]

[P]The foundation asset — Gemini AMPM Limited — has been operating since 1997, carries 13 accreditations including NSI Gold, BAFE, FIRAS and Constructionline Gold, and delivered £8.4 million net revenue and approximately £989k EBITDA in its financial year ended 30 September 2025. Gemini transitioned to Employee Ownership Trust (EOT) in October 2024, providing a genuine differentiator for public sector tendering under PPN 06/20.

[P]

[P]The Group is seeking a private equity partner to accelerate an acquisition-led growth strategy targeting £25–30 million consolidated revenue within five years. The investment ask is an acquisition facility of up to £10 million, plus £1 million of working capital to fund subsidiary launches and acqui-hire activity.

[P]3.  Market Opportunity

[P]

[P]The UK compliance services sector is experiencing a period of regulatory-driven, structural growth. Three converging forces make this an attractive moment to build a scaled platform.

[P]

[P]

[P]3.1  Building Safety Act 2022 — Mandatory Compliance Uplift

[P]

[P]The Building Safety Act 2022 introduced the most significant change to UK fire and structural safety regulation in decades. Key obligations now enforceable include:

[P]

[P]

[P]Duty holder accountability across design, construction, and occupation phases

[P]

[P]

[P]Golden thread requirement: digital documentation of all safety-critical information maintained throughout the building lifecycle

[P]

[P]

[P]Higher-Risk Building inspection and certification regime managed by the Building Safety Regulator

[P]

[P]

[P]Enhanced Remediation Orders for residential buildings — driving significant passive fire and fire door remediation programmes

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]Compliance is not optional and not discretionary. Building owners face criminal liability for non-compliance. This drives sustained, non-cyclical demand for accredited contractors with documented golden thread capability — precisely the offering AMPM Group is built around.

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]3.2  Market Consolidation — Validation of the Thesis

[P]

[P]The consolidation trend validates both the market opportunity and the exit thesis:

[P]

[P]

[P]Marlowe Group has been systematically acquiring fire safety and compliance businesses across the UK, building a £400m+ revenue platform

[P]

[P]

[P]Mitie has made multiple acquisitions in compliance and technical FM, integrating life safety within a broader FM offering

[P]

[P]

[P]Both have acquired direct competitors to Gemini AMPM, demonstrating that assets of this type trade at meaningful multiples to strategic acquirers

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]The acquirer universe for an exit event in five years is well-established and well-capitalised. AMPM Group at £25–30 million revenue, with a proprietary technology platform and an EOT heritage, is a differentiated asset within that acquirer pool.

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]3.3  Public Sector Framework Access — EOT Competitive Advantage

[P]

[P]PPN 06/20 (Procurement Policy Note: Taking Account of a Supplier&apos;s Approach to Carbon Reduction and Social Value) scores social value as a mandatory element of public sector procurement. Employee-owned businesses score materially higher than privately-owned competitors. Gemini AMPM&apos;s EOT structure provides a genuine, structural advantage in competitive tendering for:

[P]

[P]

[P]Local authority property portfolios

[P]

[P]

[P]NHS and healthcare estates

[P]

[P]

[P]Education frameworks (universities, academies, maintained schools)

[P]

[P]

[P]Social housing providers and registered social landlords

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]3.4  Sector Fragmentation — Acquisition Pipeline

[P]

[P]The compliance services market remains highly fragmented below the Marlowe and Mitie tier. There are hundreds of sub-£5 million regional operators with:

[P]

[P]

[P]Loyal client books but no succession plan

[P]

[P]

[P]Complementary accreditations (e.g., gas, water hygiene, lifts)

[P]

[P]

[P]Geographic coverage the Group does not yet hold

[P]

[P]

[P]Key operators whose relationships and contracts represent the real value

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]This fragmentation creates a favourable acquisition environment. Vendors in the £1–5 million revenue range typically trade at 3–5x EBITDA, creating meaningful value creation opportunity when integrated into a platform business that should command 6–8x on exit.

[P]8.  Technology — AMPM Live

[P]

[P]AMPM Live is a client-facing digital platform that provides building owners and facilities managers with real-time visibility of their assets, compliance status, and service delivery. It is designed to move AMPM Group from a service provider to a strategic infrastructure partner — central to how clients manage their compliance obligations.

[P]

[P]

[P]8.1  Platform Overview

[P]

[P]AMPM Live is a multi-user portal with role-based access. Each client&apos;s view is configured to their specific asset base and reporting requirements:

[P]

[P]

[P]Live asset register — every asset on site, with status, last service date, and next due date

[P]

[P]

[P]Compliance dashboard — real-time visibility of outstanding actions, overdue items, and certification status

[P]

[P]

[P]KPI reporting — customisable metrics relevant to the client&apos;s obligations (fire door inspections, detection system tests, suppression certifications, etc.)

[P]

[P]

[P]Job instruction — clients can raise reactive jobs, request quotations, or approve variations directly through the portal

[P]

[P]

[P]Document repository — all certificates, inspection reports, and remediation records stored and accessible on demand, meeting golden thread obligations under the Building Safety Act

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]8.2  Why This Matters Commercially

[P]

[P]The portal creates structural switching cost and deepens the client relationship:

[P]

[P]

[P]Once a client&apos;s asset base is onboarded to AMPM Live, the data relationship makes switching to a competitor operationally disruptive

[P]

[P]

[P]It positions AMPM as a technology-enabled partner, not a commodity trades contractor — supporting premium pricing

[P]

[P]

[P]It enables proactive compliance management rather than reactive break-fix, driving higher annual contract values

[P]

[P]

[P]Golden thread documentation within the platform directly addresses the Building Safety Act&apos;s digital record-keeping requirement, making AMPM Live a compliance tool as well as a client service tool

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]8.3  Technology Investment

[P]

[P]AMPM Live is in active development. A portion of the Group working capital fund is earmarked for platform build. The target is soft launch to existing Gemini clients in 2026, with full commercial release as a value-added service layer in 2027.

[P]9.2  AMPM Academy

[P]

[P]AMPM Academy is a planned Group entity targeted for launch in Q3 2027. It addresses a problem that is both personal and commercial: the UK compliance and building services sector has a chronic skills pipeline problem, and not enough opportunity is being created for young people entering the trades.

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]The Academy has three commercial purposes:

[P]

[P]

[P]Internal pipeline: apprentices trained to Group standards and absorbed into Gemini and the subsidiary businesses, reducing reliance on the external labour market and stabilising wage costs at scale

[P]

[P]

[P]Workforce differentiation: a trained, culturally-aligned workforce creates a service quality advantage over competitors relying on labour agencies and subcontract labour

[P]

[P]

[P]External IP licensing: accreditation pathways, training content, and regulatory compliance curricula licensed to other operators in the sector — a recurring, high-margin revenue stream that does not require headcount growth

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]Apprenticeship programmes are already running within Gemini AMPM at small scale. The Academy formalises and expands this, with a dedicated entity structure and commercial development plan.

[P]

[P]

[P]

[P]This is also a material asset in PE exit positioning. An owned training and accreditation platform adds demonstrable scarcity value to an acquirer seeking to inherit a sustainable talent pipeline, not just a client book.
