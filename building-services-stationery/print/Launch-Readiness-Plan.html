Section 1

Purpose and Critical Path

This plan tracks everything required to launch AMPM Building Services Limited cleanly on 1 July 2026. It complements the Launch Business Plan (the why and the financials) with the operational how — workstreams, owners, statuses and the decisions outstanding.

Headline

Corporate set-up, bank account and Xero are done. Commercial momentum is real — Dan has clients willing to trade. The critical path now runs through HR (SafeHR), the Integrated Management System, insurance, the inter-company people agreements and brand, all of which must land before 1 July.

Three things gate a clean launch and need a decision this week: the HR system go-live, the inter-company arrangements for seconded staff, and the insurance route. These are covered in Section 3.

Section 3

Decisions Needed Now

HR system — SafeHR go-live

Decision: confirm SafeHR is configured for Building Services before anyone transfers in. Recommendation: treat as the top pre-launch priority. No one — Steve Lee included — should move across without contracts, holiday and H&S records in place. Owner and date to be assigned this week.

Inter-company people arrangements

Decision: how Steve Lee, Joe Isaac and Jill Wilson are engaged. Recommendation: a short secondment/recharge agreement for each — Gemini invoices Building Services for their time at cost-plus. Steve Lee's move is a contractual variation or internal transfer by agreement (an intra-group move; full TUPE machinery is unlikely to be needed, but document it). This keeps Building Services carrying its own cost and stops Gemini quietly subsidising the launch — the core de-risking principle.

Insurance route

Decision: confirm with the Group broker that Phase 1 (sub-contracting into Gemini) can run under Gemini's umbrella while the entity's own EL/PL/PI is placed, and the date the standalone cover binds. Recommendation: umbrella confirmed before 1 July; own cover bound before the first Phase-2 direct instruction.

Systems — Procore and Uptick

  • Procore — extend group-wide Consistency and golden-thread documentation favour one delivery platform. Confirm whether BS is a new company in the existing account or needs added seats (cost check).
  • Uptick — defer Planned-maintenance is not a Day-1 need. Do not buy a second subscription or complicate the instance until PM contracts land.

Section 6

Insurance and Compliance Checklist

Insurance

  • Employers' Liability — statutory £5m minimum; £10m recommended.
  • Public Liability — £5m minimum; £10m for Tier-1 supply-chain.
  • Professional Indemnity — £1–2m for design-and-build / specification work.
  • Contract Works / All-Risks — required for most main-contractor appointments.
  • Plant & Equipment — if plant is owned rather than hired.

Sequence: Gemini umbrella covers Phase 1; entity's own cover binds before Phase 2. Confirm both with the broker before 1 July.

Accreditation pipeline

  • Month 1–3: NICEIC/NAPIT (electrical), Gas Safe, CHAS/SafeContractor.
  • Month 2–4: Constructionline Gold for PQQ standard.
  • Month 3–6: FMB / trade schemes; ISO alignment via the cloned IMS.

Brand and stationery

  • Logo and visual identity consistent with the AMPM Group family.
  • Letterheads, email signatures, business cards, document templates (tender, RAMS, O&M).
  • Resolve the ampmbuilding.services SSL/privacy-error and confirm the site is live for launch.
