02

Clear space & minimum size

Keep a clear margin around the lockup equal to the cap-height of the “A”. Never stretch, recolour the wordmark, or reorder the meridian.

Clear space

Exclusion zone = the height of the cap “A”. Nothing — type, rules, image edges — enters this margin.

Minimum size

Stacked lockup: 28 mm wide in print, 150 px on screen. Below this, switch to the horizontal lockup or monogram.
